Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [ADD DATE]

This Privacy Policy explains how dScribe collects, uses, and protects information when you use the dScribe Android app and related services.

1. Local-first app design

dScribe is designed as a local-first productivity app. User-created notes, todos, reminders, checklists, lists, vault entries, and similar personal content are primarily stored on your device.

2. Information you provide

You may provide information such as:

  • Account details used for sign-in (for example, email or Google account-based login).
  • Support messages you send to us, including issue descriptions and attachments.
  • Optional backup and restore choices that connect your account to backup services.

3. Information generated through app use

dScribe may process limited technical and operational information required for:

  • Authentication and account management.
  • Entitlement or subscription status checks.
  • Backup metadata and service coordination.
  • Diagnostics, crash investigation, abuse prevention, and security monitoring.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use your notes, lists, reminders, or vault content for advertising.

4. Third-party services

dScribe may use trusted third-party services to operate app features, including:

  • Supabase, for authentication, account management, entitlement/subscription status, backup metadata, and app-related backend services.
  • Google Sign-In, when you choose to authenticate with your Google account.
  • Google Drive appDataFolder, for encrypted backup and restore when you enable or connect backup.

Your use of third-party services is also subject to the terms and privacy policies of those service providers.

5. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Verify accounts and manage access to features.
  • Enable optional backup and restore workflows.
  • Investigate bugs, crashes, and security issues.
  • Respond to support requests and product feedback.

6. Data sharing

We may share limited data with service providers that help run core app features (such as authentication, infrastructure, backup coordination, and diagnostics). We do not sell personal information.

7. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect data relevant to app operation. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data retention

We retain account and operational data only as long as needed for legitimate app purposes, legal obligations, dispute resolution, or service protection. Local content remains on your device unless you choose to back it up.

9. Your choices and controls

You can typically:

  • Use core local-first features without enabling backup.
  • Choose whether to connect Google Sign-In.
  • Enable or disable backup integrations where available.
  • Contact us for account and privacy requests.

10. Children's privacy

dScribe is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date and post the revised version.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at hello@dscribeapp.com.
