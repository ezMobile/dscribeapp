Support

Need help with dScribe? Reach us at dscribe@ezmobile.in.

Help with backup & restore

Contact us if backup or restore is not working as expected, including issues with Google Drive connection or sync behavior.

Login/account help

If you are unable to sign in, link an account, or access your entitlement status, send us a support request.

Bug reports

Found unexpected behavior? Share details and steps so we can reproduce and fix the issue.

Feature requests

We welcome product ideas that improve notes, todos, reminders, checklists, and local-first workflows.

Privacy or legal questions

For privacy policy or terms questions, contact us directly by email.

Account deletion

If you created a dScribe account, you can request account deletion from the app where available or through Account Deletion.

Please include in your email

  • Device model.
  • Android version.
  • App version.
  • Description of the issue.
  • Screenshots, if relevant.

Contact: dscribe@ezmobile.in
