dScribe

Describe your thoughts. Stay organized.

dScribe is a local-first productivity app for notes, todos, reminders, checklists, lists and personal information organization.

Features

Notes

Capture ideas quickly with a focused note-taking experience built for daily use.

Todo & Reminders

Track tasks and set reminders so you can keep momentum without missing details.

Lists & Checklists

Organize grocery lists, checklists, and recurring routines in simple, reusable formats.

My Vault

Store sensitive personal notes in a dedicated space designed for privacy and control.

Google Drive Backup

Optionally backup and restore using your Google Drive appDataFolder when enabled by you.

Local-first Design

Your core content stays on your device first, helping you work reliably and privately.
